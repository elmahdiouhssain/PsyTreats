The Location

We believe that Morocco is the perfect country for our retreat. It offers a different landscape, an invitation to pause and the most wonderful cultural experience. We scoured the country to find the perfect location for you. We wanted to be close to the ocean to enable us to see a different horizon, we wanted to be near the sand to enable us to feel a different terrain underfoot and we wanted to offer you a simple yet rich backdrop to your journey alongside us. We intended to find a location suitable for exploration, with the right qualities for reflection. We welcome you to our Psychotherapy Retreat in Morocco. We know you will enjoy the colour, flavour and essence of this magical part of Africa.
