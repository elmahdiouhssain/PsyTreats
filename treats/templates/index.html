Our Idea…

We recognise the impact of stress. It affects our minds, our bodies and our spirits. It affects our confidence and our sense of worth. There is not an area of ourselves that remains unaffected when we are stressed and anxious. We decided we wanted to help our guests specifically target and manage their feelings of stress and anxiety and guide them toward seeking peace, harmony and an exciting and positive future. We have used our psychotherapy training and considered our many years of experience to enable the design of an insightful, enlightening programme cleverly tailored to each individual. Working within small groups (there are a maximum of 8 places available for each retreat), we use our unique blend of creativity, curiosity and kindness to explore in detail our feelings and responses from the very roots of their existence. We then add to the mix a good measure of understanding, some new thinking and a wealth of newly discovered confidence and we create an exciting new beginning….
