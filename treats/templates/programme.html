The Programme

We will consider and tailor our programme to accommodate your personal journey. It will begin on Day 1 of your trip and come to a close on Day 7. The programme is based on an intimate group model with a maximum of 8 guests. It is psychotherapy-lead and has been creatively designed to provide a structured exploration of your existing thinking and the narrative behind it. Our model safely allows you to understand and explore your relationships with yourself, others, and the things that cause you stress or anxiety. It will alter your approach to every aspect of your life and allow you to make significant changes to any areas you feel require difference. We use specific psychotherapeutic interventions to enable understanding and allow the challenge of existing and old behaviours where they are unhelpful. We offer space for individual and shared exploration and reflection. Our purpose is to help you discover illuminating comprehension, reliable confidence and a wonderful sense of empowerment. We intend that you leave us feeling enlightened, relaxed and positive. To enhance our programme we will offer daily yoga practice with a qualified instructor, massage therapies and wonderful, tasty Moroccan cuisine. We welcome any questions you may have about our programme or indeed any other aspect of our retreat. | Contact Us |
