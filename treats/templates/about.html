Psychodynamic & Psychotherapist



Alex is a Psychodynamic Psychotherapist. She is also qualified within the fields of Neuro Linguistic Programming and Hypnotherapy. Alex is a creative therapist and used her various qualifications and training together with her vast and varied experiences to design our programme so that it can be used flexibly with you in mind. You can expect her to meet you with her own unique blend of energy, vitality and understanding. She has a straight forward and firm intention to make things feel more comfortable and empower positive change.
